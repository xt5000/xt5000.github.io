Guacamole

Guacamole has five ingredients (sometimes only three).

Ingredients (in order of importance)

  • avocado
  • lime
  • salt
  • garlic
  • hot chile, such as serrano

Directions

  1. Peel avocado and remove seed
  2. Juice the lime
  3. Crush the garlic
  4. Crush the chile
  5. Add salt to taste
  6. Smash everything together to your liking
